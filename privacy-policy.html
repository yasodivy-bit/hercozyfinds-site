Privacy Policy — Her Cozy Finds

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Her Cozy Finds ("we", "us") operates a Pinterest account and associated automation tool that curates and shares Amazon affiliate product recommendations. This policy explains how we handle information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to this site or from Pinterest users who view our pins. Our automation tool only accesses publicly available Amazon product data and posts content to our own Pinterest account via the Pinterest API.

Use of the Pinterest API

Our application uses the Pinterest API solely to create and manage pins on our own Pinterest boards. We do not access, store, or share data belonging to other Pinterest users.

Affiliate Disclosure

Her Cozy Finds is a participant in the Amazon Associates Program. Some links in our pins are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission on qualifying purchases at no additional cost to you.

Third-Party Services

We use Amazon's Product Advertising API and Pinterest's API as part of our content workflow. Each service's use of data is governed by its own privacy policy.
